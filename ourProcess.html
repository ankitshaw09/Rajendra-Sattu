02

Traditional Sand Roasting

Unlike industrial steam processing which kills nutrients, we use the age-old sand-roasting method. This slow-heat process unlocks the rich, nutty aroma and ensures the sattu is naturally pre-cooked and easy to digest.

"The intense heat of the sand ensures uniform roasting without burning the delicate fibers of the grain."

03

Slow Stone Grinding

Our 'Chakki' stones rotate at a precise speed to prevent heat buildup. High-speed steel rollers used in factories often scorch the flour, destroying essential vitamins. Our slow method preserves the 'Praana' or life-force of the food.
